Biography

Iris van Herpen is a Dutch fashion designer known for her innovative and boundary-pushing creations. Born in 1984, van Herpen studied fashion design at the Artez Institute of the Arts in Arnhem, Netherlands, before launching her eponymous label in 2007. She quickly gained recognition for her use of advanced technologies, such as 3D printing and laser cutting, to create stunning and often otherworldly designs. Her work has been exhibited in museums around the world, including the Metropolitan Museum of Art in New York and the Victoria and Albert Museum in London, and has been worn by numerous celebrities, including Lady Gaga and Bjork. Van Herpen continues to push the boundaries of fashion design, exploring new techniques and materials to create truly unique and awe-inspiring pieces.
